# Bishal Lamichhane

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Nevada. My research interests lie in the areas of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Graph Machine Learning, and Network Science.

Recently, one area of interest in my research has been exploring the feasibility of Large Language Models (LLMs) as substitutes for human involvement in simulation-based experiments.

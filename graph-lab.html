Notes

Why this module

  • Graph-valued data does not admit a single universal Euclidean summary.
  • Choice of inference depends on the geometry, size, sparsity, and labeling regime of the graph space.
  • This prototype foregrounds path-based and neighborhood-based structure as an entry point for richer graph distances.
